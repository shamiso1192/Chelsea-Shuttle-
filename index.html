Who we are!

Chelsea Shuttles is a trusted passenger and goods transport service connecting Cape Town with major destinations across Zimbabwe. We are committed to offering safe, reliable, and comfortable travel, making long-distance journeys stress-free for our passengers. With years of experience on the road, our friendly drivers and dedicated team ensure every trip is on time and every client is treated with care.

Our services

  • Intercity travel
  • Inter-city Shuttles
  • Long distance tours
  • Inter-country logistics
  • Parcel delivery services
  • Cross-border shipping

Whether you’re travelling for business, visiting family, or sending goods home, Chelsea Shuttles is here to make the journey smooth and memorable.
